20CRv3 Diagnostics¶

How to get the version 3 data in a useable format:

  • Extracting data from 20CRv3

A collection of analyses done with the v3 data - they rely on data extracted as above, and the Meteorographica analysis package.

  • Tropical Storms
    • Super-Typhoon Nancy (1961)
    • Hurricane Carla (1961)
    • Okeechobee Hurricane (1928)
    • Galveston Hurricane (1900)
  • European windstorms
    • Lothar storm of 1999
    • Burn’s Day (Daria) storm of 1990
    • North Sea flood of 1953
    • Ulysses storm of 1903
    • Daria’s sister storm of 1884
    • Lothar’s brother (Marzorkan) of 1876
  • Extreme months
    • British Isles in October 1903
