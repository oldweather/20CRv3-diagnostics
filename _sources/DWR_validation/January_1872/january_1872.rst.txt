Validation against observations for January 1872
================================================

January 1872 is a case study where the reanalyses are very uncertain (few obs assimilated), and we are validating against a small set of stations where data was rescued by `EMULATE <https://oldweather.github.io/DWR/data/data_from_emulate.html>`_.
